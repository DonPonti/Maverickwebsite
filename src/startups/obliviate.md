### Why Obliviate?
Traditional browser history management is all-or-nothing. You either clear everything or leave your digital footprint completely exposed. 

### Core Features
Obliviate introduces granular automated rules that seamlessly wipe targeted tracking states and cookies based on semantic parameters, giving control back to the power user.
