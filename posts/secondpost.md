A rough pattern shows up once you start cataloguing companies from the region:
fintech and SaaS dominate Hyderabad, while agritech and manufacturing-adjacent
ideas show up more often out of Warangal — not surprising given the surrounding
farmland and the textile heritage.

Health-tech and deep-tech are smaller but growing categories in both cities,
often tied back to research coming out of local engineering colleges.
